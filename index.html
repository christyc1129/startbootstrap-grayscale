About my University.


PolyU Design has been consistently ranked as one of the top design schools ('The Best Design Schools in the World') in the world by BusinessWeek magazine since 2006. It is an important hub of design education and research for Hong Kong since 1964. The School building "Innovation Tower" was designed by architect Zaha Hadid, allowing us to develop skills and abilities by using various school facilities. The PolyU Design Annual Show is one of the most important event for School of Design, which all the work by graduates will be displayed and producers from design industries will choose their future employees here.
